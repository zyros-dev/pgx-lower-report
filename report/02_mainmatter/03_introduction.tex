\subsection{Including Figures}

Here is an example of how to include a figure in your report:

\begin{figure}[h]
  \centering
  \includegraphics[width=0.4\textwidth]{figures/black.png}
  \caption{Example black figure}
  \label{fig:example}
\end{figure}

You can reference the figure in text like this: See Figure~\ref{fig:example}.
